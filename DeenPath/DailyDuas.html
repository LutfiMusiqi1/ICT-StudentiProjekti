Daily Duas & Supplications

Dua is the essence of worship. This page offers authentic, easy-to-learn supplications (duas) for daily life — from waking up to sleeping, eating, entering home, and more.

What is a Dua?

Dua means calling upon Allah for help, guidance, or forgiveness. It can be made at any time, in any language, but many beautiful duas have been taught by the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ in Arabic. Sincerity and consistency are key, and making dua strengthens your connection with Allah.

Common Daily Duas

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا وَإِلَيْهِ النُّشُورُ
Alhamdu lillahil-ladhi ahyana ba‘da ma amatana wa ilayhin-nushoor
All praise is for Allah who gave us life after causing us to die, and to Him is the resurrection.
Recite upon waking up
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ
Bismillah
In the name of Allah.
Recite before eating
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَطْعَمَنِي هَذَا وَرَزَقَنِيهِ مِنْ غَيْرِ حَوْلٍ مِنِّي وَلَا قُوَّةٍ
Alhamdu lillahil-ladhi at‘amani hadha wa razaqanihi min ghayri hawlin minni wa la quwwah
All praise is for Allah who fed me this and provided it for me without any might nor power from myself.
Recite after eating
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ وَلَجْنَا وَبِسْمِ اللَّهِ خَرَجْنَا وَعَلَى اللَّهِ رَبِّنَا تَوَكَّلْنَا
Bismillahi walajna, wa bismillahi kharajna, wa ‘ala Allahi rabbina tawakkalna
In the name of Allah we enter, in the name of Allah we leave, and upon our Lord we place our trust.
Recite when entering or leaving home
بِاسْمِكَ اللَّهُمَّ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا
Bismika Allahumma amootu wa ahya
In Your name, O Allah, I die and I live.
Recite before sleeping
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْخُبُثِ وَالْخَبَائِثِ
Allahumma inni a‘udhu bika minal-khubthi wal-khaba’ith
O Allah, I seek refuge with You from all evil and evil-doers.
Recite before entering the bathroom
أَصْبَحْنَا وَأَصْبَحَ الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ
Asbahna wa asbahal-mulku lillah
We have entered a new morning and with it all dominion is Allah’s.
Recite in the morning
أَمْسَيْنَا وَأَمْسَى الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ
Amsayna wa amsal-mulku lillah
We have entered a new evening and with it all dominion is Allah’s.
Recite in the evening
