What is the Qur’an?

The Qur’an is the final revelation from Allah, sent to Prophet Muhammad ﷺ over 23 years. It consists of 114 chapters (Surahs), preserved in Arabic and translated into many languages. Its timeless guidance covers themes of mercy, justice, faith, and the oneness of God (Tawheed).

Tafsir is the explanation and interpretation of the Qur’an, while translation conveys its meaning in other languages. Both help deepen understanding for all readers.
