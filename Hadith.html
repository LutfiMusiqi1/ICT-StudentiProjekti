Sayings of the Prophet ﷺ – Hadiths

Hadiths are the recorded sayings, actions, and approvals of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ. They help us understand and implement the teachings of the Qur’an in daily life.

What Are Hadiths?

Hadiths play a vital role in Islam, providing context and practical examples for the teachings of the Qur’an. Scholars have developed a science of authentication, classifying Hadiths as Sahih (authentic), Hasan (good), or Da'if (weak). Hadiths are essential for Islamic law (fiqh) and guide Muslims in worship, ethics, and daily conduct.
